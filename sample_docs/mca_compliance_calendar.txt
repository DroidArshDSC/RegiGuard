MCA Compliance Calendar - Private Limited Companies (India)

1. Form AOC-4: Annual Financial Statement filing – within 30 days of AGM.
2. Form MGT-7: Annual Return – within 60 days of AGM.
3. Form ADT-1: Appointment of Auditor – within 15 days of AGM.
4. Form DIR-3 KYC: Director KYC – by 30th September annually.
5. Form INC-22A (ACTIVE): For active companies with registered office verification.

Late filing attracts penalties under Companies Act, 2013.
