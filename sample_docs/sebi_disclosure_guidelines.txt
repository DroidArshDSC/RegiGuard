SEBI - Disclosure Obligations for Listed Entities

Under SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015:

- Companies must promptly disclose any material event or information which could impact investors.
- Price-sensitive information must be shared with stock exchanges within 24 hours.
- Quarterly financial results must be approved by the board before publication.
- Non-compliance can attract penalties and suspension of trading.

Objective: ensure transparency, protect investor interests, and maintain market integrity.
